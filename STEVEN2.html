LEARNINGS OF INTRO TO COMPUTING

Chapter 1: Introduction to Computing

This chapter traced the historical development of computing, highlighting the major achievements and innovations that have influenced the field. It also introduced the inventors and their remarkable contributions.

Chapter 2: Professions & Careers in Computing

This chapter expanded my view on the variety of IT professions and their important roles in advancing the computing industry. I learned about the different areas of specialization within IT, each requiring specific skills and knowledge. This new understanding has helped me to see the requirements and opportunities for being a successful IT professional.

Chapters 3 and 4: Information Processing Cycle

I understood the sequential steps involved in the information processing cycle, like entering data, performing operations or processing, storing information, and retrieving it as needed. This process shows how data is transformed by computers.

Chapter 5: Basic Concepts of Computer Security

This section explored essential concepts such as keeping information confidential, ensuring its accuracy, and making it available when needed. It also looked at the challenges related to computer security, covering aspects like protecting the main system, connecting with other devices, and using databases.

Chapter 6: The Internet & World Wide Web

Investigating the story of the Internet, I learned about its components and the rules, services, and protocols that enable the cooperation of various computer elements. I comprehended the basics of Internet infrastructure and address systems. Moreover, I examined the relationship between the World Wide Web, the software that enables navigation, and the powerful servers that store web pages.

Chapter 7: Basic HTML & CSS

This module taught me how to organize content for websites using HTML5. I became familiar with HTML and gained a basic understanding of improving the appearance of web pages using Cascading Style Sheets (CSS).
